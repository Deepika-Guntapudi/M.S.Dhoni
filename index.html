M.S. Dhoni

About M.S. Dhoni

M.S. Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and captains in the history of cricket.

Achievements

Under his captaincy, India won the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007 and the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011. Dhoni is known for his calm demeanor and exceptional leadership skills.
